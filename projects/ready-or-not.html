Ready or Not is a real-life hide & seek game to play with your friends, built with Firebase and Flutter. It takes the classic game we all know and turns it into something way bigger and better.

You can play in Classic Mode, where one (or however many you want!) person seeks and everyone else hides — simple, timeless, fun. Or crank things up with Shrinking Zone, where the map gets smaller over time and hiding spots don't stay safe for long. Then there's Zombie Mode, where every person found joins the seekers, and suddenly you're being hunted by your own friends.

It's all GPS-based, super easy to set up, and perfect for parks, campuses, or anywhere you can run around a bit. Real-time tracking, smart round timers, and just the right amount of chaos.

No props, no setup — just your phone, your friends and the great outdoors.
